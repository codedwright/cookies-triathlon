The Race / VOLUNTEERS

Many hands make the load lighter.

Preparing a race for 75 participants (as well as their families) is a lot of work. And the work begins many months in advance of the actual event, too. And come race weekend, we need as much help as we can get to prep the grounds and make sure that the race is fun, well-organized and, most importantly, safe for everyone involved.

If you're not competing, or maybe you have a family member competing, please volunteer. All volunteers will receive a sweet yellow "Cook's Tri" race marshal tee.

Click here to volunteer.

We need...

  • Race Marshals
    These folks give directions to racers on the course, ensure the safety of all participants, and help enforce USAT rules.
  • Lifeguards
  • Water Station Attendants
  • First Aid Personel
  • Photographers
  • Registrars for the day of the race
  • Food Preparers
  • General labor to help set up before the race
  • and more!
