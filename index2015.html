WHERE / Jeff & Lori Cook's Home
17206 Bull Rapids Road, Spencerville, IN 46788


WHEN / Saturday, August 16, 2014
Shotgun start at 3:30PM

Cookie's Triathlon is a friends & family race hosted by Jeff and Lori Cook. The race consists of a 500-yard swim, 11-mile bike and 3-mile Run. This is a challenging course, but you don't need to be a devoted athlete who trains hours and hours each week to do it.

Yes, this is a "race" and it's fun to shoot for the top spot, but competing in this event is really about challenging yourself with a goal and working hard to accomplish it. Each year at Cook's, we see a wide range of ages, fitness levels and body types out on the course and everyone has a good time. So come challenge yourself and be encouraged by the cheers of your brothers and sisters.
