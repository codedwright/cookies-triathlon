Jeff & Lori Cook's Home
17206 Bull Rapids Road

WHEN

Saturday, August 20, 2016
Shotgun start at 3:30PM

Cookie's Triathlon is a private friends & family race hosted by Jeff and Lori Cook. The race consists of a 500-yard swim, 11-mile bike ride, and a 3.1-mile run. It's challenging, but even beginners can complete the stages. Each year we see a wide range of ages, fitness levels and body types out on the course and everyone has a good time. So come challenge yourself and be encouraged by the cheers of your brothers and sisters.
