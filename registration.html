REGISTRATION / VIA EVENTBRITE.com

Let's do this!

There is a $25 fee to register, which covers the cost of setting up the event, race schwag, and USAT-provided insurance for each participant. Not a bad deal! Registration is fast, easy and secure. We use Eventbrite.com to sign people up and process the nominal race fee. USAT Sanctioned event.

Each participant receives :

  • High quality Race Tee: Made with soft, moisture-wicking polyester stretch fabric. Features sweet Cook's Tri branded graphics. Ideal for training or race day.
  • Cookie's Triathlon Lanyard
  • More schwag.
  • Water and gatorade during the race.
  • Insurance coverage by USAT.
  • Cookout with swimming, volleyball, fireworks, etc. after the race!

Note: Required race gear like a bicycle or helmet, etc. will not be provided. Participants who do not have the required may not participate according to USAT rules.
