Technology

There's a ton of technology to help you set goals and track your progress as you achieve them. Check out a few of these resources.

RunKeeper.com Great way to track your mileage.

NIKE+ Integrates your iPod a running shoes.

MapMyFitness.com Apps for your smartphone.
